question: Which method works best for solving $x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0$?
A: Quadratic formula
B: Factoring ✓
C: Completing the square
D: Graphing
explanation: The numbers −2 and −3 multiply to 6 and add to −5, so factoring is quickest.
